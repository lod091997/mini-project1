About Us

TATAK /ta·tak/ n.

MEANS: trademark

Sining /si·ning/ n.

MEANS: art

At TATAKSINING, we cherish the age-old tradition of handcrafting, weaving stories into every creation. Our journey began amidst the tranquil waters of Philippine waterways, where the abundance of water lilies sparked our innovative spirit. What was once considered a nuisance has become our canvas for creativity and sustainability.
